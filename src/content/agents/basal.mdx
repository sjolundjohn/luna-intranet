Basal is Luna's first shared agent — a chat assistant you can DM in Slack like any colleague. Each Lunite gets their own private conversation with its own history, so you don't have to re-explain context every time.

## How to use it

### In Slack

1. Open Slack and search for **Basal** in the sidebar (or the `@` mention list).
2. Click the app to open a direct-message thread.
3. Type your question the way you'd ask a person. You can be informal — Basal is not picky.
4. Follow-ups work. Basal remembers the last several messages in your thread.

You can also mention **@Basal** in a channel Basal has been added to — handy for quick lookups in front of the team. Channel mentions don't carry over to your DM thread.

### On this site

Scroll to the [Try it](#try) section below to chat with Basal right here, no Slack required. The responses are the same, but the trial conversations aren't saved — each refresh starts over.

## What to ask

Basal is good at:

- **Drafting and editing.** "Rewrite this email in a warmer tone." "Punch up this paragraph."
- **Summarizing.** Paste a long thread, a meeting transcript, a doc, or a blog post and ask for the key points.
- **Brainstorming.** "Give me five angles on …" or "What are the arguments against …"
- **Explaining.** "Explain continuous glucose monitoring like I'm new to diabetes." "What's a FDA 510(k)?"
- **Analysis of content you paste in.** Comparing two documents, pulling numbers out of a table, identifying patterns.
- **Writing code.** Small scripts, config snippets, SQL queries. Not a replacement for a real dev environment, but useful for one-offs.

Basal is less good at:

- **Real-time information.** It doesn't browse the web. Recent news, today's calendar, latest pull requests — Basal doesn't see those yet.
- **Luna-specific knowledge.** For now Basal only knows what's in its general training data. It can't read our internal documents. (Retrieval over Luna's documents is on the roadmap.)
- **Remembering across threads.** Your DM thread has memory, but context doesn't cross between threads or channels.

## A few useful prompts

- _"Rewrite this to be one paragraph and half the length: \[paste\]"_
- _"I'm about to send this email to a regulator. Tell me what's unclear or could be misread."_
- _"Summarize this meeting transcript in three bullet points and then list any open questions: \[paste\]"_
- _"I'm designing a survey about device comfort. Suggest five short questions a user could answer in under a minute each."_

## Privacy & security

- **Your conversations are private.** Other employees cannot see your messages with Basal.
- **Luna-owned infrastructure.** Basal runs on Luna's own Cloudflare infrastructure. It is not a third-party SaaS product.
- **No AI training on your data.** Basal is powered by Claude. Zero Data Retention is enabled on Luna's Anthropic workspace, meaning Anthropic does not use our interactions to train its models.
- **Audit logging.** All interactions go through Cloudflare's AI Gateway and are logged for security and observability. The log captures metadata (timing, tokens, cost), not message content.
- **Identity verification.** Your identity is confirmed through your Slack account's `@lunadiabetes.com` email address.

## Don't put in Basal

Don't paste protected health information, patient identifiers, or any confidential third-party data covered by NDA. Basal is an internal productivity tool, not a cleared system for regulated data.
