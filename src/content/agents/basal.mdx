Basal is Luna's first shared agent. You talk to it in Slack the same way you'd talk to a colleague — direct message the **@Basal** app, or mention it in a channel it's been added to. It has its own conversation history with you, so you don't need to re-explain context every time.

## What Basal can help with today

- **General questions and brainstorming.** Ask Basal to help you think through a problem, generate ideas, or explore a topic.
- **Writing and editing.** Draft emails, refine copy, rewrite paragraphs for clarity, or adjust tone.
- **Summarizing information.** Paste in long text and ask for a summary or the key takeaways.
- **Analyzing data or documents.** Paste content directly into the chat and ask Basal to analyze, compare, or extract insights.
- **Company knowledge questions.** Basal can answer questions based on its general training data. Dedicated access to Luna's internal documents is coming soon.

## How to talk to Basal

Open Slack and DM **@Basal** like any other person. Your messages are private — other employees can't see your conversations. You can ask follow-up questions, and Basal will remember what you said earlier in the thread.

## Privacy & security

- **Your conversations are private.** Other employees cannot see your messages with Basal.
- **Luna-owned infrastructure.** Basal runs on Luna's own Cloudflare infrastructure. It is not a third-party SaaS product.
- **No AI training on your data.** Basal is powered by Claude, Anthropic's AI model. Zero Data Retention is enabled, meaning Anthropic does not use Luna's interactions to train its models.
- **Audit logging.** All interactions are logged for security and observability purposes.
- **Identity verification.** Your identity is confirmed through your Slack account's `@lunadiabetes.com` email address.

## Don't put in Basal

Don't paste protected health information, patient identifiers, or any confidential third-party data covered by an NDA. Basal is an internal productivity tool, not a system cleared for regulated data.
