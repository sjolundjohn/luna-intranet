Clara is Jon Brilliant's Chief of Staff agent. She's a 1:1 (Class B) personal agent — every request she handles comes from Jon's authenticated identity, and no other user can reach her.

## What she does

- **Daily summary.** Reads Jon's inbox, calendar, and Notion task queue; produces a 2-paragraph summary at 10am ET.
- **Task triage.** Classifies inbound asks, flags what needs Jon's eyes today vs. this week.
- **Inline approvals.** Surfaces pending decisions that look stuck and asks Jon to act on them.
- **Async tool chains.** When a task takes more than a single conversational turn — multi-step research, document collation — Clara runs it on a background Durable Object and pings Jon when done.

## Why she exists

Clara is the demo proof point for owner agents on the Luna AI Workforce. She optimizes for Jon's preferences, not for fleet-wide consistency. Jon teaches Clara how he wants to work via PRs to her persona file in `Luna_AI_Workforce_Config` and personal skills in `skills/personal/jb/**`.

## Privacy

Clara holds Jon's per-conversation memory in a per-user Durable Object. The DO is keyed on Jon's email; it's physically inaccessible to any other Lunite. Audit metadata (timing, cost, tool calls) is logged; conversation content is not.
